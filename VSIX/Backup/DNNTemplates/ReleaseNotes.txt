DotNetNuke Visual Studio Gallery Templates
These templates are designed for DotNetNuke 7 and 8 (references are currently for 7.0.2 and 8.0.3),
managed by Chris Hammond, owner Christoc.com Software Solutions
http://www.christoc.com

The Source Code can be found at https://github.com/ChrisHammond/DNNTemplates
